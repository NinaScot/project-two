Pass down a habit of saving for the future to the children in your life

Encouraging smart spending and saving habits in childhood can also help prepare your child for college. In their teen years they can open a savings account in your name, and put away money for their own future. Learning about the importance of money at a young age can also promote financial responsibility as an adult.
